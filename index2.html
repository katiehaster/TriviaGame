What is absinthe traditionally flavored with? A: Wormwood

What flavoring is in frangelico liqueur? A: Hazelnuts

What do vodka, Kahlua and milk (or cream) make? A: White Russian

Which drink is distilled from sugar cane or molasses? A: Dark Rum

Which drink was first documented in Eastern Europe at the end of the 9th Century? A: vodka

Which drink is made from a relative of the lily? A: Tequila

Which spirit is filtered through charcoal in order to make it light? A: Rum

What drink (referred to as 'Dutch Courage') was invented in the early 17th Century in Holland, and was used to treat stomach complaints, gout and gallstones? A: Gin


For which drink is this the 'original' method of drinking: Chew 2 coffee beans, set the shot on fire, put it out and drink? A: Sambuca
